# Mobile_Sales_Dashboard-Power BI
This project demonstrates my ability to design and develop a comprehensive BI dashboard that provides actionable insights into mobile phone sales. The dashboard was created using Power BI and showcases multiple dimensions of sales performance, including geography, product models, payment methods, and customer ratings.
<img src="Mobile_Sales_Main_Dashboard cmplt1.png" width="800">
## Objectives
- Analyze mobile sales trends across cities, months, and days of the week.
- Identify top-performing mobile models and brands.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Evaluate customer satisfaction through ratings.
## Tools & Data
- Tool Used: Power BI
- Dataset: Mobile sales data (simulated for analysis)
- Techniques: Data modeling, DAX calculations, interactive filters, and visualization design.
## Key Features
- KPIs: Total Sales (68M), Total Quantity (2K), Transactions (331), Average Price (41K).
- Geographic Analysis: Sales by city across India, highlighting Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, and others.
- Time Trends: Monthly quantity trends and daily sales patterns.
- Payment Insights: Distribution of transactions by UPI, Credit Card, and Debit Card.
- Customer Ratings: Breakdown of Good, Average, and Poor ratings.
- Top Models: Apple iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, and Samsung Galaxy S21 among leading models.
## Insights & Impact
- City Performance: Delhi and Mumbai emerged as top contributors to sales.
- Payment Preferences: UPI was the most popular payment method, reflecting digital adoption.
- Model Popularity: Apple and OnePlus models dominated sales, indicating strong brand loyalty.
- Sales Timing: Tuesdays recorded the highest sales, useful for planning promotions.
## Value Delivered
This dashboard enables stakeholders to:
- Make informed decisions on inventory allocation.
- Tailor marketing strategies to high-performing cities and models.
- Optimize payment offerings based on customer preferences.
- Monitor customer satisfaction to improve service quality.
